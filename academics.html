Academic Excellence

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our academic programs are designed to challenge students while supporting their individual learning needs and growth.

At Khair Un Nass, we follow a rigorous curriculum that combines traditional educational values with modern teaching methodologies. Our approach focuses on developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a lifelong love for learning.

  • National curriculum with enhanced content
  • Individualized learning plans
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Dedicated counseling support
Our Programs

Academic Levels

Primary School

Grades 1-5 | Ages 6-11

Building strong foundations through play-based learning and structured academic activities.

  • Basic literacy and numeracy
  • Introduction to sciences
  • Art and music education
  • Physical education
  • Moral education

Middle School

Grades 6-8 | Ages 11-14

Developing critical thinking and preparing students for advanced academic challenges.

  • Advanced core subjects
  • Computer literacy
  • Foreign languages
  • Project-based learning
  • Study skills development

High School

Grades 9-12 | Ages 14-18

Preparing students for university and career success with advanced academics.

  • Specialized tracks
  • SAT/University preparation
  • Research projects
  • Career counseling
  • Leadership opportunities

Subject Areas

Sciences

Mathematics

From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, building analytical and problem-solving skills.

Languages

English, Pashto, Dari, and optional foreign languages for comprehensive communication skills.

Arts
