How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with all required information about your child.

2

Document Verification

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous grades, and health records.

3

Assessment Test

Your child will take an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their academic level.

4

Interview

Both parents and student will meet with our admissions team for a personal interview.

5

Admission Decision

You will receive notification of the admission decision within 5-7 business days.

6

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by paying fees and attending orientation.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year start?

Our academic year begins in March and ends in December, with summer break from mid-July to mid-August.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 20:1 to ensure personalized attention for each student.

Do you offer transportation?

Yes, we offer transportation services for students within Kabul. Please contact our office for routes and fees.

Is there a waiting list?

Due to high demand, some grades may have waiting lists. We recommend applying early to secure a spot.

What about school uniforms?

Yes, uniforms are required. Uniforms can be purchased from our designated supplier. Details will be provided upon enrollment.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely! We welcome prospective parents to tour our campus. Please contact us to schedule a visit.

Have Questions?

Our admissions team is here to help. Contact us for more information about the application process.
