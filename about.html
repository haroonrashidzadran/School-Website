Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2004, Khair Un Nass Private High School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Afghanistan for over two decades.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with Islamic values and modern pedagogical approaches. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to one of the most respected schools in the region.

2004

Foundation

Khair Un Nass was established with just 50 students and 5 dedicated teachers.

2008

Expansion

Added middle school program and expanded facilities to accommodate growing enrollment.

2012

Recognition

Received regional recognition for academic excellence and innovative teaching methods.

2020

Modernization

Launched state-of-the-art computer labs and digital learning platforms.

2024

Present Day

Serving over 1200 students with 45 dedicated educators and comprehensive programs.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that empowers students to achieve academic excellence, develop strong moral character, and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces confident, creative, and compassionate individuals prepared to meet the challenges of a changing world while upholding traditional values.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Innovation, and Service. We instill these core values in everything we do, preparing students for lifelong success.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Khair Un Nass

Dear Parents and Students,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Khair Un Nass Private High School. For over two decades, our institution has been committed to providing quality education that nurtures the whole child - mind, body, and character.

At Khair Un Nass, we believe that every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered. Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to create an environment where students feel supported, challenged, and inspired to achieve their best.

We combine rigorous academics with character education, ensuring that our students not only excel academically but also develop into responsible, compassionate individuals who will make positive contributions to their communities.

I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the opportunities that await your child at Khair Un Nass.

Dr. Ahmad Shah

Principal
